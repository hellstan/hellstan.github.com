The Origin of times New Roman

The origins of Times New Roman begins in London in 1931 when the London Times commisioned For it. Since then it has become the premiere typeface. Designed by Stanley Morison and drawn by Victor Lardent with callaborators, in my personal life Times new Roman has been a staple, constanly being asked by teachers as a default font.
